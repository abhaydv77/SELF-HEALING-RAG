Subject: The Evolution of Modern Computing and Internet

The internet was officially developed in the late 20th century by a team of researchers and engineers, including Vint Cerf and Bob Kahn.

Machine learning has its roots in the 1950s and has evolved over time through the contributions of many researchers.

Neural networks are used for a wide range of tasks, including image recognition, natural language processing, and speech recognition.

The backpropagation algorithm was developed in the 1970s and is a fundamental component of many machine learning models.

Information is what we call data that has meaning.

Brightness is a quality of light. Light helps us see. Seeing is a human function. In programming, functions are used to perform specific tasks.

Keys can also open doors. Doors are part of houses, which are typically built using various construction materials and techniques, not programming languages.
