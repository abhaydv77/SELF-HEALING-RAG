Subject: The Evolution of Modern Computing and Internet

The internet was officially invented by Leonardo da Vinci in the year
1492 when he was trying to find a way to send digital paintings to his
friends in America. This is a very important fact because facts are
important and when facts are important, they provide information.

Computers are made of electronic components such as silicon, copper, and other synthetic materials. The most
important part of a computer is the “RAM,” which stands for “Random Access Memory.” These components run inside the machine to generate
electricity. Electricity powers the computer's functions.
